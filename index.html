Cucumber Cooler

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 22.5ml fresh lime juice
  • 15ml sugar syrup
  • 15ml soda water
  • 2 slices of peeled cucumber
  • 8 mint leaves

Instructions

  • Muddle cucumber in the base of a shaker
  • Add gin, lime juice, sugar syrup and mint
  • Shake with ice and fine strain into chilled glass
  • Top with splash of soda

Gin & Juice

Ingredients

  • 60ml Dimpsey Somerset Gin
  • 30ml grapefruit juice
  • Sliced grapefruit

Instructions

  • Combine gin and grapefruit juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice
  • Stir well and strain into a chilled martini glass
  • Garnish with grapefruit slice
